❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change the language?

Go to the Settings tab and select your preferred language (English or Spanish). All content updates instantly.

How do I search for a saint?

Go to the Saints tab and use the search bar. You can search by name, interest, country, or patron association.

How do I filter saints?

On the Saints tab, use the filter chips below the search bar to filter by age, gender, life state, or region.

Can I suggest a saint to add?

Yes! Use the contact form above and select "Suggest a Saint." We'd love to hear your ideas for future updates.

Does this app collect my data?

No. The app collects zero personal data. Everything works offline and your preferences stay on your device. Read our Privacy Policy.

Is there an Android version?

Not yet, but it's planned! The app is designed for cross-platform content sharing, so an Android version is on the roadmap.
