Confirmation Saints — Support

About the App

Confirmation Saints helps Catholic confirmation candidates discover and choose a patron saint for their Confirmation journey. Browse saints by name, interest, region, feast day, and more — all in English and Spanish.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change the language?

Go to the Settings tab and select your preferred language (English or Spanish).

How do I search for a saint?

Go to the Saints tab and use the search bar at the top. You can search by name, interest, country, or patron saint association.

How do I filter saints?

On the Saints tab, use the filter chips below the search bar to filter by age, gender, life state, or region.

Can I suggest a saint to add?

Yes! Please contact us at the email below and we'll consider adding them in a future update.
