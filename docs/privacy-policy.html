Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 13, 2026

Confirmation Saints App

Confirmation Saints ("the App") is developed by Jorge Balderas. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

All saint content, images, and educational material are bundled within the App itself. The App does not connect to the internet, does not use analytics, and does not include any advertising or tracking frameworks.

Local Storage

The App stores the following preferences locally on your device only:

  • Language preference — your chosen display language (English or Spanish)
  • Onboarding status — whether you have completed the welcome screen

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party. You can reset this data at any time by deleting the App.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services, SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

The App is designed for Catholic confirmation candidates, which may include users under 18. Since the App collects no personal data whatsoever, it is safe for users of all ages.

External Links

The App contains links to external websites (source citations for saint information). These links open in your device's web browser. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external websites.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
